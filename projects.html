My Final Solo

On April 16th, 2024, I performed my final high school solo for the Academy of Visual and Performing Arts (AVPA). I performed a solo for the public every year as a member requirement of the Academy of Visual and Performing Art. Reflecting back, it was a long and incredible journey to that point, yet it felt as if no time had passed since my first solo. I will be performing Toccata in D minor by J.S. Bach, but you might know it as the piece that plays during lightning in a 1900s horror film, Dracula.
